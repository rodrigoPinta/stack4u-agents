Responsible AI & Governance

Stack4U builds financial AI agents designed to support professionals, not replace human judgment.

Our agents are advisory by design: they analyze, flag risks, and explain findings, while final decisions always remain with humans.

We follow a governance-first approach inspired by established enterprise risk-management principles, prioritizing transparency, accountability, and control.

All agents operate with clear scope boundaries, conservative behavior when uncertain, and full explainability of their outputs.

Source financial data is never altered, and critical actions always require human approval.

This approach ensures Stack4U's AI remains trustworthy, auditable, and aligned with the serious nature of financial work.
